Privacy Policy


Privacy Policy for CarGame
Effective Date: 17/06/2023
This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which CarGame, developed by Loopix, collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (referred to as "Players" or "you") of the CarGame application. This policy applies to the CarGame application and all services offered by Loopix.


  1. Information Collection

    CarGame may collect pseudonyms and friends list information from Players for the purpose of inviting other players to game sessions and creating a leaderboard. No personally identifiable information, such as names, email addresses, or addresses, is collected.

  2. Use of Epic Online Services SDK

    CarGame utilizes the Epic Online Services SDK (Software Development Kit) for login functionality, game sessions, and leaderboard management. Please refer to Epic Online Services' privacy policy for information on how they handle user data.

  3. Age Restrictions

    CarGame does not impose any age restrictions on players. The game is open to players of all ages.

  4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    CarGame does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies to collect user data.

  5. In-App Purchases

    CarGame does not offer any in-app purchases. The game is free to play without any additional purchases required.

  6. Analytics

    CarGame employs Epic Online Analytics to track player behavior and game performance. This helps us improve the gaming experience but does not involve the collection of personally identifiable information.

  7. Data Collection Outside the Game

    CarGame does not collect any data from users outside the game. This Privacy Policy only applies to information collected within the CarGame application.

  8. Contact Information

    If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the data handling practices of CarGame, please contact us at contact@loopix.me.

  9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

    Loopix reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Players are encouraged to check this page regularly for any changes. By continuing to use CarGame, you acknowledge and agree to the terms outlined in the updated Privacy Policy.

    By installing and using CarGame, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
